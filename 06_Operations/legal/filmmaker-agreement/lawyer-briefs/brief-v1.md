# Lawyer Brief — Filmmaker Representation Agreement

**Status:** Draft (not yet sent to lawyer)

## About SI8

SuperImmersive 8 (SI8) is an AI film distribution agency based in Taipei, Taiwan.
We represent AI filmmakers globally and license their work to streaming platforms,
educational institutions, festivals, and brands — primarily in APAC and Europe.

We are a new company (founded 2026). This will be our standard representation agreement.

---

## What We Need

A Filmmaker Representation Agreement that is:
- Governed by Taiwan (ROC) law
- Enforceable for international (non-Taiwan) filmmakers
- Appropriate for non-exclusive representation
- Suitable for AI-generated content with specific IP warranties

---

## Key Terms

| Term | SI8 Position |
|------|-------------|
| Exclusivity | Non-exclusive |
| Territory | APAC + Europe (with option to expand) |
| Duration | 12 months, auto-renews with 30-day notice to cancel |
| Commission | 25% of deals brokered by SI8 |
| Payment to filmmaker | 75%, paid within 30 days of SI8 receiving payment |
| Works covered | Filmmaker's choice (specific titles or all AI films) |
| Filmmaker approval | Required on exclusive licensing deals |

---

## AI-Specific Clauses Needed

Please draft warranty clauses where filmmaker confirms:

1. All AI tools used in production were used in compliance with those tools' terms of service
2. No unauthorized real-person likeness, voice, or identity was used
3. Content does not infringe known copyrights to filmmaker's knowledge
4. Filmmaker will disclose all AI tools used upon SI8's reasonable request
5. Indemnification: filmmaker indemnifies SI8 against claims arising from their content
