Tool Risk Tiers (February 2026)

Not all AI tools carry equal risk. SI8 classifies tools into four risk tiers based on training data transparency, litigation status, and commercial terms.

✅ Recommended

Adobe Firefly, Runway (paid), Pika

Safest options — Adobe Stock-trained (Firefly) or best commercial terms with documented service improvement clauses.

⚠️ Monitor

Sora (paid)

Best IP terms (Disney/Marvel/Pixar licensed; others opt-in only), but legal pushback building. SI8 continues to monitor.

⚠️ Caution for Exclusivity

Kling

Retains permanent sublicensable license to use outputs for AI training. Approved for catalog but flagged for exclusivity deals.

❌ Prohibited

Veo (consumer), Hailuo/Minimax, Midjourney, Stability AI

Veo consumer = no commercial use. Hailuo/Minimax = active Disney/WB lawsuit (Nov 2025). Midjourney/Stability = studio litigation.
