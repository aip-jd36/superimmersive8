How Safe Lane Works

Every piece of AI video content in our catalog passes through a rigorous 4-stage vetting process. We do the legal homework so brands and platforms don't have to.

01

Pre-Screen (15 minutes)

Quick gate before investing full review time:

  • Are all tools on Approved or Caution list?
  • Commercial plan receipts provided?
  • Any obvious No List violations?
  • Modification rights authorization complete?
  • Audio disclosure complete?
Outcomes: Proceed / Request Info / Reject
02

Full Review (45-60 minutes)

Seven review categories with clear thresholds:

  • Tool & Plan Verification — Paid receipts, approved tools
  • Human Authorship Evidence — 150+ word declaration quality
  • Likeness & Identity Scan — No real-person faces/voices
  • IP & Brand Imitation — No copyrighted characters
  • Brand Safety Assessment — Mainstream commercial suitability
  • Audio & Music Rights — Licensed or original AI only
  • Modification Rights — Tier 2 placement authorization
03

Risk Tier Assignment

Content classified into clear risk categories:

✓ Certified

Adobe Firefly primary tool, no caution-tier tools

✓ Standard

Runway / Pika / Sora (paid) — approved tools, no prohibited tools

⚠ Caution-Flagged

Kling used — approved but noted; affects exclusivity pricing

✗ Reject

Prohibited/high-risk tool as primary generation source

04

Decision & Output

Final determination and documentation:

Clean Pass → Generate Rights Package → Assign catalog ID → Add to catalog
Conditional Pass → Filmmaker provides missing docs (14 days) → Re-review
Reject → Written explanation + resubmission guidance

The No List — What SI8 Won't Work With

Hard boundaries that protect filmmakers, clients, and SI8 from highest-risk areas:

✗ Celebrity Likeness

Real people's faces without consent

✗ Voice Cloning

Real person voices without authorization

✗ IP Imitation

Copyrighted characters, brands, trademarks

✗ Deepfakes

Deceptive content pretending to be real

✗ Political Content

Persuasion or advocacy content

✗ CMI Stripping

Watermark/attribution removal (DMCA violation)

✗ Prohibited Tools

Veo consumer tier, tools in active litigation

✗ No Human Authorship

Prompts alone (not copyrightable per USCO)

Review Turnaround Time

Under 90 minutes per submission

Target total time from submission to decision. Conditional passes (requiring additional documentation) may extend to 5 business days for filmmaker response and re-review.
