Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Standard and Extended?
Standard License is for digital and social use (1 year, non-exclusive). Extended License adds broadcast/TV rights, longer terms, global territory, and category-exclusive options.
Can I upgrade my license later?
Yes. Contact us to upgrade from Standard to Extended, or to explore Custom Placement options for existing catalog assets.
What's included in the Chain of Title?
Every asset comes with a structured documentation package covering tool provenance, model disclosure, Rights Verified sign-off, commercial use authorization, modification rights status, category conflict log, territory information, and version history. Learn more
What if I need exclusive rights?
Category-exclusive rights are available in Extended and Custom Placement tiers. This means your brand will be the only one in your product category to license that specific asset. Contact us for a custom quote.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes. Contact us for multi-asset licensing (5+ clips) and we'll create a custom package with volume pricing.
How does Custom AI Placement work?
We take an existing catalog production and use AI to regenerate specific scenes with your brand elements integrated. This gives you custom branded content at a fraction of traditional production cost, with full Chain of Title documentation. No reshoot required.
