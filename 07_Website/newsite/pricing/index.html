FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Payment is required upfront before review begins.
Do you offer refunds?
No refunds, but if your video is rejected, we provide a clear explanation and offer free resubmission after you make corrections. Most rejections occur due to missing documentation (e.g., missing commercial plan receipts) or prohibited tool usage.
How long does verification take?
Up to 5 business days. Most reviews are completed within 2-3 days. The actual review takes ~90 minutes, but we allow buffer time for queue management.
Can I verify multiple videos at once?
Yes. Each video is $499. Submit each video separately through our platform. Volume discounts (5+ videos) will be available in Year 2.
What if my video is rejected?
We provide a detailed explanation of why your submission didn't meet our criteria. In many cases, you can correct the issue (e.g., provide missing receipts, update authorship declaration) and resubmit at no additional cost.
What tools do you NOT verify?
We do not verify content created with prohibited/high-risk tools including: Hailuo (training data concerns), Veo (access restrictions), Midjourney (image-only), or tools used on free/non-commercial plans. See our full tool tier list in the Rights Playbook.
Do I have to opt into the Showcase catalog?
No. Catalog opt-in is completely optional. Many creators just want the verification documentation for their own client work. If you do opt-in, you can remove your work from the catalog at any time.
