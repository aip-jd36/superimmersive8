FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Creator Record and SI8 Certified?
Creator Record ($29) is fully automated — you fill out the form, the system generates a PDF stamped "SELF-ATTESTED — NOT FOR COMMERCIAL USE." It's a structured record for your own files, portfolio, or small-client work. SI8 Certified ($499) involves a 90-minute human review of your submission, verifying receipts, screening for IP and likeness issues, and producing a certified "CLEARED FOR COMMERCIAL USE" document that satisfies brand legal teams and E&O insurance requirements.
Can a brand or agency use the Creator Record ($29) document?
No. The Creator Record is explicitly stamped "NOT FOR COMMERCIAL USE" and is not accepted by brand legal teams or E&O underwriters as a verified compliance document. For any commercial application — ads, sponsored content, streaming distribution, licensing — SI8 Certified is required.
Where do short films for streaming or distribution go?
SI8 Certified. A streaming platform licensing your short film has the same legal exposure as a brand using it in a commercial. Distribution liability is determined by who is buying, not the format of the content.
Can I upgrade from Creator Record to SI8 Certified later?
Yes. Log in, find your submission, and request an upgrade. You'll pay the $499 fee and your submission enters the human review queue. No need to fill out the form again.
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Payment is required upfront before review begins.
How long does SI8 Certified verification take?
3–5 business days. Most reviews complete in 2–3 days. The actual review takes ~90 minutes; we buffer for queue management.
Do you offer refunds?
No refunds. If your SI8 Certified submission is rejected, we provide a detailed explanation and offer free resubmission after corrections. Most rejections are for missing receipts or prohibited tool usage — both correctable.
What tools are not eligible for verification?
We do not verify content created with prohibited tools including: Hailuo (training data concerns), Veo (access restrictions), Midjourney (image-only, no video), or any tool used on a free or non-commercial plan. See our Rights Playbook for the full tool tier list.
Do I have to opt into the Showcase catalog?
No. Many creators use SI8 Certified purely for their own client documentation. Catalog opt-in is entirely optional and available only on SI8 Certified. You can opt out at any time from your dashboard.
