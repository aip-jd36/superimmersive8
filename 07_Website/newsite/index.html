FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What AI tools do you verify?
We verify commercial-grade AI video tools including Sora, Runway, Kling, Pika, and others. Each tool is reviewed for commercial use authorization and training data safety. Adobe Firefly is also supported. We do NOT verify tools with unclear IP policies or those trained on unlicensed content.
What if my video is rejected?
If your submission doesn't meet our criteria, we'll provide a detailed explanation. In some cases, you can resubmit with corrections. Rejections typically occur when tools weren't used with paid commercial plans, or when there's clear IP imitation. We're here to help you understand what needs to change.
How long does verification take?
Our target turnaround is 5 business days. Most reviews are completed within 90 minutes of review time, but we allow buffer for queue management. Rush processing may be available—contact us for details.
Do I have to opt into the marketplace?
No. The marketplace is completely optional. Many creators just want the verification documentation for their own client work. If you do opt-in, you can remove your work from the Showcase at any time.
How does the 80/20 commission split work?
If you opt into our Showcase marketplace and a brand licenses your work, you receive 80% of the licensing fee. SI8 keeps 20% for handling negotiations, contracts, and payments. You'll be notified of every deal and receive payment within 30 days of the deal closing.
