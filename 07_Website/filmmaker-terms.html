Filmmaker Representation Terms

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Important: By submitting your work through the SI8 Rights Verified intake form, you agree to the following terms. Please read carefully before submitting.

1. Agreement Overview

This agreement is between you (the "Filmmaker") and SuperImmersive 8 ("SI8", "we", "us"). By submitting your AI-generated film or video content through our Rights Verified intake process, you agree to these terms.

2. Non-Exclusive Representation

You retain full ownership and copyright of your work. SI8's representation is non-exclusive. You are free to:

  • License your work directly to other parties
  • Work with other agents or representatives
  • Use your work for any purpose
  • Distribute your work through any channels you choose

SI8 only earns commission on licensing deals that we facilitate. You are under no obligation to accept any licensing opportunities we present.

3. Catalog Display Rights

You grant SI8 a non-exclusive, worldwide license to:

  • Display your work (including thumbnails, still frames, and video previews) on the SI8 catalog at superimmersive8.com
  • Use your work in marketing materials, promotional campaigns, and pitch decks to attract potential licensees
  • Share your work with prospective buyers (brands, platforms, agencies) for licensing consideration
  • Create derivative works solely for the purpose of brand integration (if you have authorized Tier 2 modification rights)

Your work will only be added to the public catalog if it passes our Rights Verified review process.

4. Commission Structure

SI8 earns 20% commission on licensing deals we facilitate. You earn 80%.

Commission applies to:

  • All licensing fees paid by buyers for use of your work
  • Upfront fees and ongoing royalties (if applicable)
  • Tier 1 (as-is licensing) and Tier 2 (custom AI product placement) deals

SI8 only earns when you earn. There are no upfront fees, submission fees, or review fees. If your work doesn't license, SI8 doesn't earn anything.

Commission does not apply to deals you secure independently without SI8's involvement.

5. Rights Verified Review Process

Upon submission, SI8 will review your work against our Rights Verified criteria within 5-7 business days. The review assesses:

  • Tool provenance and commercial plan verification
  • Human authorship evidence
  • Likeness and identity compliance
  • IP and brand imitation risks
  • Brand safety suitability
  • Audio and music rights
  • Modification rights authorization (for Tier 2 eligibility)

Possible outcomes:

  • Clean Pass: Your work is added to the catalog and available for licensing
  • Conditional Pass: Additional documentation required (you have 14 days to provide)
  • Reject: Your work does not meet Rights Verified criteria. We will explain why and whether resubmission is possible.

Passing the Rights Verified review does not guarantee licensing opportunities. SI8 makes no promises about revenue, licensing deals, or market demand.

6. Modification Rights (Tier 2 AI Product Placement)

If you authorize modification rights in your submission, you grant SI8 permission to:

  • Commission AI-regenerated versions of your work with brand elements integrated (e.g., product placement, branded environments, logo integration)
  • Modify specific scenes or the full work as needed for brand integration
  • License these modified versions to brands for commercial use

Important clarifications:

  • You control the scope of modification (full work or specific scenes)
  • You retain approval rights over brand integrations (final approval required before licensing modified versions)
  • All modifications must maintain the creative integrity of your original work
  • You can revoke modification rights at any time with 30 days written notice (existing licensed works remain subject to their agreements)

If you do not authorize modification rights, your work is only eligible for Tier 1 (as-is licensing). You can update your modification authorization at any time by contacting SI8.

7. Representations and Warranties

By submitting your work, you represent and warrant that:

  • You own all rights to the submitted work or have proper authorization from all rights holders
  • Your work does not infringe any third-party copyright, trademark, right of publicity, or other intellectual property rights
  • All AI tools used were accessed through paid commercial plans that permit commercial licensing
  • Your work does not contain real-person likenesses, voices, or identifiable individuals without proper consent
  • All information provided in your submission is accurate and complete
  • You have the authority to enter into this agreement

8. Territory and Exclusivity

Unless otherwise specified in your submission:

  • Territory: Global (worldwide licensing)
  • Exclusivity: Non-exclusive for Tier 1 (as-is licensing); Category-exclusive for Tier 2 (brand placement)

Category-exclusive means: if Brand A licenses your work for product placement in the "beverage" category, we will not license the same work to competing beverage brands. However, we can license it to brands in other categories (e.g., automotive, technology).

You can specify territory restrictions or exclusivity preferences in your submission.

9. Payment Terms

When SI8 facilitates a licensing deal:

  • SI8 will invoice the buyer and collect payment on your behalf
  • Your 80% share will be paid within 30 days of SI8 receiving payment from the buyer
  • Payment will be made via bank transfer, PayPal, or other mutually agreed method
  • You are responsible for any taxes owed on your earnings

10. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice to the other party.

Effect of termination:

  • Your work will be removed from the SI8 catalog within 30 days
  • SI8 will stop pitching your work to new buyers
  • Existing licensed works remain subject to their licensing agreements (SI8 continues to earn commission on these deals)
  • You retain all rights to your work

Termination does not affect any obligations or liabilities incurred before termination.

11. Limitation of Liability

SI8 provides representation services and Rights Verified review on an "as is" basis. We make no guarantees about:

  • Licensing opportunities or revenue
  • Complete legal risk elimination (our review reduces risk but does not eliminate it)
  • Third-party claims or disputes

SI8's liability is limited to the commission earned on the specific transaction giving rise to the claim. SI8 is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

You agree to indemnify and hold SI8 harmless from any claims arising from your work, including claims that your work infringes third-party rights or violates laws.

12. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This agreement is governed by the laws of Taiwan. Any disputes will be resolved through:

  1. Good faith negotiation between the parties
  2. If negotiation fails, binding arbitration in Taipei, Taiwan

SI8 may update these terms from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email. Continued use of SI8's services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
