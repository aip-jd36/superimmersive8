How Rights Verified Works

90-minute expert review | $499 per video | 5-day turnaround

What is Chain of Title Verification?

Chain of Title documentation proves the legal lineage of AI video content — who created it, how it was made, and whether it's safe for commercial use.

Why Brands Need It

"Won't get you sued" is the Year 1 positioning. Brands want to use AI video (Sora, Runway, Kling) but legal teams block deployment due to three risk categories:

  • Copyright Infringement: Training data lawsuits, output similarity claims
  • Regulatory Compliance: FTC disclosure rules, state AI laws, platform violations
  • Copyrightability Uncertainty: Can the brand actually own the AI-generated commercial?

What You Get

9-field Chain of Title documentation package (PDF) that reduces legal exposure and enables distribution:

  1. Tool Provenance Log
  2. Model Disclosure
  3. Rights Verified Sign-off
  4. Commercial Use Authorization
  5. Modification Rights Status
  6. Category Conflict Log
  7. Territory Log
  8. Regeneration Rights Status
  9. Version History

How It Works: 4-Stage Process

01

Submit Your Video

Complete our intake workflow covering four key areas:

  • Tool Disclosure — which AI tools were used, versions, and commercial plan confirmation
  • Human Authorship — your creative direction, prompt strategy, and editorial decisions
  • IP & Likeness Confirmation — no real persons, no copyrighted characters or brands
  • Production Metadata — title, runtime, genre, audio source, supporting receipts

02

We Review (90 minutes)

Stage 1: Pre-Screen (15 min)

  • Are all tools on Approved or Caution tier list?
  • Are commercial plan receipts provided?
  • Any obvious No List violations?
  • Is modification rights authorization complete?
  • Is audio disclosure complete?

Outcomes: Proceed / Request Info / Reject

Stage 2: Full Review (45-60 min)

Seven review categories with clear pass/fail thresholds:

  1. Tool & Plan Verification — Paid receipts, approved tools
  2. Human Authorship Evidence — 150+ word declaration quality
  3. Likeness & Identity Scan — No real-person faces/voices
  4. IP & Brand Imitation Scan — No copyrighted IP
  5. Brand Safety Assessment — Suitable for commercial attachment
  6. Audio & Music Rights Verification — Original/licensed only
  7. Modification Rights Scope — Full work vs. scene-level

03

Risk Tier Assignment

  • Certified: Adobe Firefly as primary tool, no caution-tier tools
  • Standard: Runway / Pika / Sora (paid plans) — approved tools, no prohibited tools
  • Caution-flagged: Kling used → approved but noted in Chain of Title
  • Reject: Any prohibited or high-risk tool used as primary generation source
04

Decision & Chain of Title Generation

Clean Pass:

  • Generate 9-field Chain of Title (PDF)
  • Assign catalog ID (SI8-2026-####)
  • Add to internal catalog
  • Email creator: "Your video is approved! Download your Chain of Title."

Conditional Pass:

  • Request missing documentation
  • Filmmaker has 14 days to provide
  • Re-review after submission

Reject:

  • Written explanation of why
  • Guidance on whether resubmission is possible
  • Free resubmission after corrections

What We Check: 7 Vetting Categories

Clear standards remove ambiguity. Human expertise reserved for genuine gray areas.

1. Tool & Plan Verification

Every AI tool used has a paid commercial license and is on the Approved or Caution tier list.

2. Human Authorship Evidence

Declaration demonstrates meaningful human creative direction — sufficient to establish human authorship, not just prompt entry.

3. Likeness & Identity

No identifiable real person's face, voice, or identity without documented consent. Hard line, no exceptions.

4. IP & Brand Imitation

No copyrighted characters, protected trade dress, or trademarked identifiers without authorization.

5. Brand Safety

Content suitability for commercial brand attachment. Which brand categories are appropriate.

6. Audio & Music Rights

Every audio element is original AI-generated with commercial license, licensed with documentation, or absent.

7. Modification Rights

Filmmaker authorized SI8 to commission AI-regenerated brand-integrated versions for product placement deals.
