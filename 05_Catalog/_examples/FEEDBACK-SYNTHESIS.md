## Required Fixes Before Public Release

### 🚨 CRITICAL: Legal Language (Both AIs Flagged)

#### Issue 1: Training Data Assertions
**Current problem:**
- States "trained on licensed stock footage, public domain content..." as if SI8 verified it
- References "Runway's public model card" but readers may interpret as SI8 validation

**ChatGPT fix:**
- Change to: "Tool provider states in its published materials that..."
- Add: "SI8 does not independently audit training datasets."

**Action needed:** Soften training data language to "per tool provider's public documentation"

---

#### Issue 2: "Certified By: SI8 Review Team"
**Current problem:**
- "Certified" is strong legal language
- Can be read as a promise/warranty despite disclaimers elsewhere

**ChatGPT fix options:**
1. Replace "Certified" with "Reviewed / Verified / Documented"
2. Or keep "certified" but explicitly define it as: "certification = completion of SI8 Rights Verified checklist, not legal certification"

**Gemini agrees:** Certification language is risky

**Action needed:** Replace "Certified" with "Reviewed and Documented" throughout

---

#### Issue 3: "No legal red flags identified"
**Current problem:**
- Can sound like a legal guarantee

**ChatGPT fix:**
- Keep the statement but add: "This reflects SI8's internal risk criteria as of the package date; it is not a legal determination."

**Action needed:** Add clarifying sentence to soften legal determination language
