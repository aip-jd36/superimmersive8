## Product Positioning

**NOT:** "We're advisors" or "We're consultants"
**YES:** "We built the Rights Verified system for AI video. Here's how to apply it to your workflow."

**Target customer:** Singapore/APAC agencies building AI content in-house who need **process, not content**.

**Value proposition:** Reduce time from creative idea to legal approval from 3 weeks to 3 days.

---

## Two Product Offerings

### Option A (Entry): $2,000 - $3,500

**Format:** Single engagement (1-2 weeks)

**Deliverables:**
1. **60-90 min strategic call**
   - Review one campaign concept
   - Review intended distribution channels (YouTube, TikTok, Meta, etc.)
   - Identify risk hotspots based on SI8 Rights Verified criteria

2. **1-Page Risk Memo** (PDF)
   - Go/No-Go recommendation for specific campaign
   - Top 3 risk areas flagged
   - Platform-specific disclosure requirements

3. **AI Video Disclosure Checklist** (template)
   - What to document for each AI tool used
   - When disclosure is required vs. optional
   - Internal approval workflow suggestions

4. **"What to Document" Template**
   - Tool provenance log format
   - Receipt filing system
   - Human authorship declaration template

**Pricing:**
- $2,000 base (single campaign review)
- $3,500 (includes 1 follow-up call + 30-day email support)

**Timeline:** 7-10 business days

---

### Option B (Premium): $5,000

**Format:** 2-week engagement

**Deliverables:**
1. **Everything in Option A, PLUS:**

2. **Platform Policy Map** (tailored to their distribution channels)
   - YouTube AI content policy (current requirements)
   - TikTok synthetic media policy
   - Meta transparency requirements
   - Netflix/streaming platform guidelines (if relevant)
   - Comparison table showing policy differences

3. **Internal Approval SOP** (process document)
   - Step-by-step review workflow for agency's legal/compliance team
   - Stakeholder sign-off checklist
   - Risk tier assignment (Certified / Standard / Caution-flagged)
   - When to escalate to external counsel

4. **AI Video Disclosure Language Templates**
   - On-screen disclosure text (multiple platform variants)
   - Video description disclosure language
   - Client contract rider language
   - Social media post disclosure templates

5. **"AI Video Compliance Pack v1"** (branded deliverable)
   - All above documents in single PDF package
   - Agency can use internally for multiple projects

6. **2 follow-up calls** (30 min each)
   - Week 2: Mid-engagement check-in
   - Week 4: Post-implementation Q&A

**Pricing:** $5,000 flat fee

**Timeline:** 2 weeks engagement + 2 follow-up calls over 4 weeks
