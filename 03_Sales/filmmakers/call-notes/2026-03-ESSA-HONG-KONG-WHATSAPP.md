## Key Learnings

### 1. **Commission Split Is Too High (80/20 Problem)**

**What JD pitched:**
- Initial pitch: "20% royalty on licenses" (sounds like filmmaker gets 20%)
- Later clarified: **"We take 80%"** (meaning filmmaker gets 20%, SI8 takes 80%)

**Industry standard:**
- Literary/film agents: 10-15%
- Music publishers: 15-25%
- Stock photo (Getty/Shutterstock): 15-45% (not 80%)
- Talent agents: 10-20%

**⚠️ 80% agency commission is VERY aggressive.**

**Why this is problematic:**
- $5,000 deal → Filmmaker gets $1,000, SI8 gets $4,000
- Filmmaker: "I did all the creative work, you get 4x what I get?"
- **This will be a hard sell to filmmakers**

**Essa's reaction:** Asked detailed questions about economics, suggesting skepticism about the split.

## What Needs to Change (Supply-Side)

### ❌ **Current Model Issues:**

1. **80/20 split is too aggressive**
   - Industry standard: 10-25% agency commission
   - 80% will scare away professional filmmakers
   - Only works if SI8 provides massive value (distribution, marketing, buyers lined up)

2. **Performance-based only = risk for filmmaker**
   - No upfront fee = no guaranteed income
   - Filmmakers prefer hybrid: small upfront + royalty

3. **Positioning unclear**
   - Getty (marketplace) vs Curation agency (representation) are different business models
   - Need to pick one and commit

4. **No proof of concept**
   - MyVideo is first AI film program (unproven)
   - No existing catalog to show filmmakers
   - Hard to recruit without case studies

---

### ✅ **Potential Fixes:**

**Option 1: Lower Commission Split**
- **Standard agency model:** SI8 takes 20-25%, filmmaker keeps 75-80%
- Easier to recruit filmmakers
- Need higher deal volume to hit revenue targets

**Option 2: Hybrid Payment Model**
- Small upfront fee ($200-$500) to add to catalog (covers Rights Verified vetting cost)
- PLUS royalty split (80/20 or 70/30)
- Filmmaker gets guaranteed income, SI8 recoups vetting costs

**Option 3: Tiered Commission (Based on Deal Size)**
- Small deals (<$1K): 50/50 split
- Medium deals ($1K-$5K): 70/30 split (SI8 takes 30%)
- Large deals (>$5K): 80/20 split (SI8 takes 20%)
- Scales with value delivered

**Option 4: Prove Concept First with MyVideo**
- Close MyVideo deal
- Document case study (filmmaker made $X, got Y views, etc.)
- Use success story to recruit more filmmakers
- "Here's proof it works"
