## Message 1: Professional/Detailed

**Best for:** Professional creators with polished portfolios
**Tone:** Formal, emphasizes process credibility

```
Hey [First Name] — The [specific project] is exceptional.

We're closing a licensing deal with a major Asian streaming service for vetted AI
content. They need tool provenance + chain of title documentation — exactly what
we provide. Your work would be a great fit.

15-min call? Link in bio.
```

**Personalization tokens:**
- `[First Name]` - Use their actual first name
- `[specific project]` - Reference their recent work (e.g., "cyberpunk cityscape piece", "the forest animation from last week")

## Response Handling Scripts

### If they reply: "Tell me more"

```
Great! Here's the quick version:

We're SI8 — AI film rights agency. We're closing a deal with a streaming platform
in Asia (Taiwan-based, think Netflix-style curation).

They need vetted AI content (3-10 min shorts). We handle the rights verification
(tool provenance, chain of title, brand safety). You keep rights (non-exclusive),
earn 20% royalty on licenses.

Next step: 15-min call to see if your work is a fit. [Calendly link]
```

---

### If they ask: "Which platform?"

```
Leading streaming service in Taiwan/Asia — can share more on a call, but they're
looking for premium short-form AI content (3-10 min) for their curated tier.

Think Netflix-style curation, not user-generated platform. They have strict rights
requirements, which is why they need our vetting process.
```

---

### If they ask: "What's the pay?"

```
Platform licensing typically $1,500-$3,000 per film depending on runtime/exclusivity.
You get 20% royalty.

Plus you keep rights to license elsewhere (non-exclusive), so you can still monetize
through other channels. We only earn when you earn (20% commission on deals we bring).
```

---

### If they ask: "What's the catch?"

```
No catch. We need to run your work through our Rights Verified process first:
- Tool provenance check
- Rights verification
- Brand safety review

Takes about a week. If it passes, we add to catalog and pitch to the platform.

If it doesn't pass, we'll explain why (usually fixable — like need commercial plan
receipts, or audio rights documentation).
```

---

### If they ask: "Why me?"

```
Honestly? Your work is strong, and you're using the right tools (Runway/Sora/Kling
paid plans = platform-acceptable).

Platform is looking for quality over quantity. They'd rather license 20 exceptional
films than 200 mediocre ones. Your [specific work] hits the mark.
```

## Pre-Send Checklist

Before sending ANY message:

- [ ] Liked 2-3 of their recent posts first (warm up connection)
- [ ] Verified they use professional tools (Runway/Sora/Kling/Pika paid plans)
- [ ] Confirmed they post original work (not just reposts)
- [ ] Checked for existing representation (bio mentions agent/manager)
- [ ] Found specific work to reference in personalization

---

## Red Flags - DON'T Message If:

- ❌ Only reposts others' work (not original creator)
- ❌ Works full-time at Runway/Pika/OpenAI (conflict of interest)
- ❌ Bio says "not taking offers" or similar
- ❌ All content is amateur/hobbyist quality
- ❌ Uses only free tools or prohibits commercial use
