## Channel Strategy

| Channel | Target | Why |
|---------|--------|-----|
| **LinkedIn** | Creative Directors, Heads of Production, EPs at agencies | Searchable by job title + company size; B2B buyer ICP |
| **Instagram** | AI filmmakers, independent creators, AI video artists | Where AI creators actually live and post their work; no equivalent LinkedIn presence |

**LinkedIn campaigns are agency-only.** Creator campaigns live here.

---

## ICP (Instagram Creator)

**Primary:**
- AI filmmakers making short-form work with Sora, Runway, Kling, Hailuo, Pika
- Independent directors and animators with AI video in their portfolio
- Artists with commercial ambition — festival submissions, distribution interest, licensing goals
- Using paid/commercial tool plans (signal: Runway Standard or Pro, not free tier)

**Secondary:**
- Boutique studio founders making AI content for themselves or clients
- Social creators transitioning to longer-form AI narrative work

**Green flags:**
- Posts original AI work regularly
- Mentions specific tools in captions/bio
- Has festival credits or platform interest signals in bio
- Verified account or 5K+ followers

**Red flags (don't message):**
- Only reposts others' work
- Free tool users only (no commercial plan)
- Bio says "not taking offers"
- Amateur/hobbyist quality only
- Works at a tool company (Runway, Pika, OpenAI, etc.)

---

## Pre-Send Checklist

Before sending ANY DM:
- [ ] Liked 2-3 of their recent posts (warm up)
- [ ] Verified they use commercial tools (paid plans mentioned in posts/bio)
- [ ] Confirmed original creator (not repost account)
- [ ] Found specific work to reference
- [ ] Checked bio for representation / "not taking offers" flags
