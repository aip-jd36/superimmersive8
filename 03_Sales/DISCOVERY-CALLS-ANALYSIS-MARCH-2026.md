## Executive Summary

**Buyer-Side (Demand):**
1. **Wrong buyer type targeted** — Media buying agencies ≠ content buyers; need creative agencies/production houses
2. **Rights concerns validated in Singapore** — Mid-size agencies care about legal risk (contradicts small agency pattern)

2. **No upfront fee = no conversion** — Filmmakers want guaranteed income, not performance-based only

4. **Social proof > perfect economics** — Need 1-2 case studies before scaling recruitment

**Critical Blocker:** MyVideo uncertainty + lack of social proof are stalling both sides of the marketplace.

#### Stock Platform Industry Standards (Actual Research)

**Getty Images / iStock:**
- **Non-Exclusive:** Creator gets 15% (photos), 20% (video) — Platform keeps 80-85%
- **Exclusive:** Creator gets 25-45% — Platform keeps 55-75%

**Shutterstock:**
- **Standard tier:** Creator gets 15% — Platform keeps 85%
- **Top tier (1,000+ licenses/year):** Creator gets 40% — Platform keeps 60%

**Source:** `/05_Catalog/creators/PAYOUT-MODEL.md` (February 22, 2026)

---

#### Why Stock Platforms Take 60-85%

Because they provide:
- Global marketplace with millions of buyers
- Enterprise sales teams
- Legal infrastructure & indemnification
- Hosting, search, payment processing
- Marketing spend (Google Ads, SEO, partnerships)

---

#### SI8's 80/20 Split: Industry-Standard Rate, But Unproven Distribution

| Platform | Creator Gets | Platform Keeps | Distribution Proven? |
|----------|--------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Getty iStock (video)** | 20% | 80% | ✅ Yes (millions of buyers) |
| **Shutterstock (standard)** | 15% | 85% | ✅ Yes (millions of buyers) |
| **SI8 (Tier 1)** | 20% | 80% | ❌ No (0 proven buyers) |

**The Real Problem:**
- **80/20 is NOT too aggressive compared to Getty/Shutterstock**
- **80/20 IS too aggressive for an unproven platform**

**What Getty Provides for 80%:**
- Millions of monthly buyers
- $450 average transaction value
- Proven passive income (contributors see monthly payouts)

**What SI8 Provides (Year 1):**
- 0 proven buyers
- MyVideo deal = "maybe"
- No case studies, no social proof

**Essa's Implicit Question:**
> "You want Getty's commission rate, but do you have Getty's distribution?"

#### 4. Positioning Confusion (Marketplace vs Agency)

**JD Described Two Different Models:**

**Model 1 (Marketplace):**
> "Getty Images for AI videos. You submit to our catalog, we verify the rights, provide proof document, and then pay you a royalty if a buyer purchases."

**Model 2 (Agency/Curation):**
> "I'm curating a short film series for them [MyVideo]"
>
> "We take 80% for finding the clients, handling the curation and representation, and the rights vetting."

**These Are Different Business Models:**

| Aspect | Marketplace (Getty) | Agency (Representation) |
|--------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Commission | 15-25% creator payout | 10-20% agent commission |
| Volume | High (thousands of assets) | Low (curated roster) |
| Service Level | Self-serve | High-touch, bespoke deals |
| Relationship | Transactional | Ongoing partnership |
| Example | Getty, Shutterstock | CAA, WME |

**Correct Positioning:**
- SI8 Tier 1 = **Stock Platform** (Getty model: 80/20 split)
- SI8 Tier 2 = **Production Partner** (50/50 split for active work)
- SI8 is NOT a talent agency (10-20% commission)

**Messaging Consistency Required:**
- Always position Tier 1 as "stock platform like Getty"
- Always cite Getty 20% video rate as benchmark
- Never use "agency representation" language (implies 10-20% commission)

- Shutterstock (standard): Creator gets 15%, platform keeps 85%

✅ **The REAL problem: SI8 hasn't earned Getty-level commission yet**
- Getty can justify 80% because they deliver millions of buyers
- SI8 can't justify 80% without proven distribution

✅ **Three valid paths forward:**
1. Prove distribution first, then recruit at Getty rate
2. Offer tiered commission (50% → 30% → 20% as SI8 proves distribution)
3. Pay upfront + Getty rate (hybrid model)

✅ **Recommended: Option 2 (Tiered Commission)**
- Breaks chicken-egg immediately
- No upfront cash required
- Path to Getty rate once proven
