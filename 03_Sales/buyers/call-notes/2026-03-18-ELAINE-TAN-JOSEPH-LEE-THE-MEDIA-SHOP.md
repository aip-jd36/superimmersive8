---

### 2. **Rights Concerns Exist, But Solved at Production House Level**

**Agencies DO care about rights** (Elaine mentioned concerns multiple times).

**BUT:** They solve it by trusting production houses, not by licensing pre-vetted catalog content.

**Current model:**
- Agency → hires production house → production house clears rights → agency trusts them

**Where SI8 fits:**
- Production house → uses SI8 Rights Verified process → documents clearances → gives Chain of Title to agency

**This is B2B2B, not B2B:**
- SI8 sells to production houses (B2B)
- Production houses sell to agencies (B2B)
- Chain of Title passes through to end client

**Elaine's objection:** *"Every product has different objectives, different target audiences... So how does this platform allow a luxury brand to choose the creative from there that will fit your brand image."*
