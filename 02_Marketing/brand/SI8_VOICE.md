## Who SI8 Is

**SuperImmersive 8 (SI8)** is a B2B compliance infrastructure company for AI video content, based in Taipei. SI8 issues Chain of Title documentation for AI-generated video — accepted by brand legal teams and E&O underwriters for commercial deployment.

SI8 is not a creative agency. Not a law firm. Not a platform for hobbyists. It is the verification layer between AI video creation and commercial use.

**The one-line positioning:** "CarFax for AI video."

**The competitive frame:** "Adobe verifies Firefly only. We verify the tools your legal team is blocking."

**The infrastructure frame:** "Rights clearance infrastructure for AI media."

## Tone Principles

**Direct.** Lead with the point. No warm-up sentences, no "In today's world..." openers. The first sentence must carry weight.

**Authoritative, not arrogant.** SI8 speaks from a position of domain expertise. State what is true. Don't hedge unnecessarily, but don't overreach into legal guarantees.

**Specific over vague.** Use numbers, dates, agency names, dollar figures, regulation names when available. "E&O insurers are excluding AI video" is weaker than "94% of ASA enforcement actions now come from proactive AI scanning."

**One idea per post.** If it needs two points, it's two posts. Complexity kills engagement.

**Problem-first.** Lead with what's broken or changing in the market, then connect to what SI8 solves. Don't lead with SI8 — lead with the pain.

**No filler.** Cut: "I'm excited to share", "In recent news", "It's no secret that", "As we all know", "Game-changer", "Groundbreaking", "Revolutionary."

**No exclamation marks** unless quoting someone else.

**No em-dashes (—)** in posts. Use a period or restructure the sentence.

## What SI8 Talks About

SI8 posts are grounded in one of four angles:

1. **Regulatory signal** — A new law, enforcement action, or industry standard that tightens AI content requirements. Connect to Chain of Title as the compliance response.

2. **Market signal** — A brand, insurer, or platform changing their AI content policy. Connect to the documentation gap SI8 fills.

3. **Industry gap** — Something the market is getting wrong about AI compliance (e.g. confusing C2PA provenance with legal clearance). SI8 corrects the record.

4. **Product proof** — A specific thing SI8 does that solves a concrete problem. Show, don't tell.

---

## What SI8 Does Not Talk About

- Creative trends, AI aesthetics, "the future of storytelling" — not SI8's lane
- Specific client names or deals without permission
- Legal advice or guarantees ("this will protect you from lawsuits")
- Competitor takedowns — acknowledge the category, not attack players
- Anything that implies SI8 certifies copyright clearance as a law firm would

## Language Rules

**Always say:**
- "SI8 Certified" (not "SI8 certified" — capital C)
- "Creator Record" (not "creator record")
- "Chain of Title" (not "chain of title" or "COT")
- "SuperImmersive 8" or "SI8" (never "Super Immersive 8" or "SuperImmersive8")
- "documentation" or "verification" (not "rights-cleared" — implies legal warranty SI8 cannot back up)

**Never say:**
- "rights-cleared" — implies a legal guarantee
- "cleared for commercial use" as a statement of fact — this is what the stamp says, not a claim SI8 makes in marketing
- "game-changer", "revolutionary", "groundbreaking"

## LinkedIn Post Structure (Company Page)

**Length:** 3–6 sentences. No headers or bullets in the post body — this is prose, not a listicle.

**Structure:**
1. **Signal sentence** — state the news or market development plainly (1 sentence)
2. **Gap sentence** — explain why this creates a documentation or compliance problem (1–2 sentences)
3. **SI8 connection** — connect to what SI8 provides, specifically (1–2 sentences)
4. **Implication** — direct statement of what this means for the target reader: agency, brand, or production house (1 sentence)

**No call-to-action required** in every post. SI8 builds authority through accurate observation, not constant conversion prompts. Add a CTA only when genuinely relevant.

**Example (good):**
> The EU AI Act's transparency requirements take effect August 2, 2026. Brands deploying AI video in EU markets will need documented proof of tool provenance and disclosure compliance — not just internal records, but audit-ready documentation that travels with the file. C2PA metadata tells you what tool made the content. It does not tell you whether the output is safe to run commercially. SI8 Certified fills that gap: a structured 90-minute audit producing Chain of Title documentation accepted by brand legal teams and E&O underwriters.

**Example (bad):**
> Exciting news in the AI space! The EU AI Act is now live and it's a game-changer for content creators. We at SI8 are here to help you navigate these complex regulations and make sure your content is compliant. Reach out to learn more!

## Target Reader

When writing any SI8 post, write for one of these three:

1. **Agency MD or Creative Director** — runs a 10–50 person agency, producing AI video for brand clients, starting to get questions from clients about documentation
2. **Brand Marketing Lead** — responsible for AI content approvals, has been told by legal to "sort out the AI documentation thing"
3. **Production House Producer** — mid-career, has delivered AI video projects, not yet burned but aware the compliance conversation is coming

Write as if they are competent professionals, not beginners. Don't explain what AI video is. Explain what the compliance gap means for their specific situation.

## Confidence Level

SI8 speaks with earned confidence. The regulatory tailwinds are real (IAB Jan 2026, EU AI Act Aug 2026, ASA enforcement). The documentation gap is real. SI8 does not need to hedge or qualify its existence.

That said: SI8 does not overstate its legal authority. It provides structured documentation and audit process — not legal advice, not a litigation shield. Posts should reflect this: confident about the problem, precise about what SI8 provides.
