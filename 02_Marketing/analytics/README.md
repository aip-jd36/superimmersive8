# Analytics & Metrics

## Email List KPIs (track monthly)
- Total subscribers
- Monthly growth (new subscribers)
- Open rate (target: >35% for niche B2B newsletter)
- Click rate (target: >5%)
- Unsubscribe rate (flag if >2% per issue)

## Website KPIs (track monthly)
- Unique visitors (Risk Briefing page specifically)
- Email capture conversion rate (visitors → subscribers)
- Top traffic sources

## Content KPIs (track per piece)
- White paper downloads
- Lead magnet conversion rate
- LinkedIn post engagement (impressions, saves, shares)

## Review cadence
Monthly — 30 min check against targets.
