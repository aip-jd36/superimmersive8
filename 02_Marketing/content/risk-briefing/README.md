# Risk Briefing — Content & Update Log

Source content for the Risk Briefing section of the SI8 website.

## What This Is
A curated, periodically updated page on superimmersive8.com summarizing the current AI content legal and platform risk landscape. 6-8 entries. Each entry has a headline, 2-3 sentence SI8 commentary, source link, and date.

## Update Cadence
When something significant happens — roughly monthly/quarterly. Not a news feed.
Always update the "Last Updated: [Month Year]" timestamp on the page.

## Files
- `BRIEFING-CONTENT.md` — current website page copy (source of truth)
- `UPDATE-LOG.md` — running log of what was added/changed and when
