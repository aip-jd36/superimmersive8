## The Problem

Brands are using AI-generated video in campaigns. Most content comes from social feeds with **zero commercial rights documentation**.

Legal teams are blocking AI content because:
- ❌ No proof of commercial license from AI tool providers
- ❌ Unknown training data sources (potential IP infringement)
- ❌ No verification that real people's faces or voices weren't used
- ❌ Platform policies reject undocumented AI content

**Your creative team loves AI video. Your legal team won't touch it.**
