## Draft

TikTok can't enforce its own AI disclosure rules.

Samsung skipped them entirely. Other brands are doing the same.

This is the part nobody is talking about: platforms are mandating AI labeling but have no reliable way to detect AI content at scale — even when brands don't comply.

So who's on the hook when an unlabeled AI video causes a regulatory problem?

The brand. The agency. The production house that signed off on it.

"We followed the platform's policy" isn't a legal defense when the platform can't verify its own policy is being followed.

This is exactly why Chain of Title documentation exists — not because TikTok needs it, but because your legal team does.
