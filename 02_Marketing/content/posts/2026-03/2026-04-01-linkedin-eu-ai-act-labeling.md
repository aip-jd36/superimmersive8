## Draft

The EU AI Act's Code of Practice on AI content labeling just dropped.

The short version: if you're deploying AI-generated video content in the EU, you need to be able to prove it's labeled correctly — and that you knew what it was before you ran it.

This is no longer a platform policy question. It's a regulatory compliance question.

The brands that are prepared have documentation that answers three questions:
1. Was this content AI-generated?
2. Which tools made it?
3. Was it disclosed appropriately before deployment?

That's exactly what Chain of Title documentation covers.

For agencies working with EU clients — or any brand running campaigns in EU markets — this is the moment to put a documentation process in place before a regulator asks for one.

The Code of Practice is still in draft. The direction is clear.
