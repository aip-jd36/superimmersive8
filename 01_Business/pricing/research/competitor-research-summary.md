## Premium Stock Video Agencies

### 1. Filmsupply
**Website:** filmsupply.com
**Model:** Premium cinematic stock footage representing high-end human filmmakers

**Pricing Structure:**
- **Digital/Web Use:** $500 - $2,500 per clip
- **Broadcast/TV Rights:** $5,000 - $15,000+ per clip
- **License Type:** Royalty-free with usage tiers

**Why This Matters to SI8:**
- Closest aesthetic/quality analogue to SI8's catalog
- If human-made cinematic stock is $1,500 digital, AI with legal docs should be similar or higher
- Shows premium stock video market exists above commodity tier

**Source:** Peer review research (Gemini), February 2026

---

### 2. Getty Images (Rights-Managed Video)
**Website:** gettyimages.com
**Model:** Premium stock with Rights-Managed licensing for exclusive/high-value content

**Pricing Structure:**
- **Royalty-Free (RF):** $125 - $500 per clip (à la carte)
- **Rights-Ready (RR):** Starting at $99 for limited distribution, up to thousands for broad/broadcast
- **Rights-Managed (RM) Digital/Corporate:** $800 - $3,000 depending on audience size, duration
- **UltraPacks:** Bundled credits $130 - $499 per download
- **Premium Access:** Annual subscription (custom pricing, contact sales)

**Why This Matters to SI8:**
- Industry standard for premium stock licensing
- RM pricing ($800-$3,000 digital) validates SI8's $1,500-$3,000 range
- Shows market accepts custom pricing for broadcast/commercial rights

**Sources:**
- [Getty Images Plans and Pricing](https://www.gettyimages.com/plans-and-pricing)
- [Getty Images Pricing Explained - Photutorial](https://photutorial.com/getty-images-pricing/)

### 3. Shutterstock (Video)
**Website:** shutterstock.com
**Model:** Volume-based subscription stock video marketplace

**Pricing Structure:**
- **Subscription:** Flex subscriptions with videos at 10 credits each (any resolution: SD, HD, 4K)
- **Pricing Tiers:** $79-$669/month for 5-20 downloads/month
- **Lowest Cost:** $8.32/video (largest annual prepaid plan)
- **Video Packs:** Prepaid downloads valid 1 year (alternative to subscription)
- **License:** Standard Video License (unlimited views on digital platforms, web, social, online ads)

**Why This Matters to SI8:**
- NOT a direct competitor (commodity/volume model)
- Shows what SI8 is NOT competing with
- Validates premium positioning ($1,500 vs. $8-30 subscription)

**Sources:**
- [Shutterstock Video Pricing 2026](https://www.stockphotosecrets.com/buyers-guide/shutterstock-pricing.html)
- [Shutterstock Video Plans](https://www.shutterstock.com/pricing/video)
- [Shutterstock Video Pricing Guide - Footage Secrets](https://www.footagesecrets.com/buyers-guide/shutterstock-video-pricing/)

### 4. Adobe Stock (Video)
**Website:** stock.adobe.com
**Model:** Stock video integrated with Creative Cloud, tiered licensing

**Pricing Structure:**
- **Subscription Plans:** $29.99 - $249.99/month (1-750 assets or 1-25 videos)
- **Credit Packs:** $64 - $188/video (on-demand)
- **License Upgrades:**
  - Enhanced License: $79.99 - $199.99 per asset (unlimited print, broadcast)
  - Extended License: Premium tier for merchandise/resale
- **License Types:**
  - Standard: Digital, social, print (up to 500K copies)
  - Enhanced: Unlimited print, broadcast, templates
  - Extended: Merchandise, resale products

**Why This Matters to SI8:**
- Shows clear licensing tier structure (Standard → Enhanced → Extended)
- Enhanced license premium ($80-200) validates broadcast rights markup
- License upgrade model = potential SI8 add-on strategy

**Sources:**
- [Adobe Stock Pricing 2026](https://www.stockphotosecrets.com/buyers-guide/how-much-are-adobe-stock-images.html)
- [Adobe Stock License Types Explained](https://www.stockphotosecrets.com/questions-answers/licensing/adobe-stock-license.html)

### 5. Pond5
**Website:** pond5.com
**Model:** Marketplace for stock video, à la carte + subscription options

**Pricing Structure:**
- **À la carte:** Starting at $39/clip
- **Subscription Plans:** 10 downloads/month options available
- **License Types:**
  - Individual License: Standard digital/web use
  - Business License: Regional broadcast (tri-state area, local news)
  - Premium License: National/global distribution required

**Why This Matters to SI8:**
- Shows tiered license structure by use case (digital → regional → national)
- $39 floor shows commodity end of market (not SI8's target)
- Premium pricing for broadcast validates SI8's Extended tier

**Source:** [Pond5 Pricing and Licenses](https://www.pond5.com/pricing)

## Rights Clearance Agencies

### 9. Catch&Release
**Website:** catchandrelease.com
**Model:** UGC/viral video finder + legal clearance + indemnification service

**Pricing Structure:**
- **Clearance fee + licensing fee:** $1,500 - $4,000 for digital usage

**Why This Matters to SI8:**
- **THE closest structural analogue to SI8**
- Proves agencies pay $2,000+ purely for "legal shield" and paperwork
- Validates Chain of Title as premium value (legal risk mitigation)
- Shows market exists for "vetting + documentation" premium over raw content

**Source:** Peer review research (Gemini), February 2026

## Pricing Patterns & Insights

### License Type Progression (Across Platforms)

| License Type | Use Cases | Typical Price Range |
|--------------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Standard/Basic** | Digital, social, web, internal use | $8 - $500 (stock) / $1,500+ (premium) |
| **Enhanced/Business** | Broadcast, unlimited print, regional | 2-5x markup ($80-200 upgrade) |
| **Extended/Premium** | Merchandise, resale, global, exclusive | 5-10x markup ($500-5,000+) |

**SI8 Alignment:**
- Standard: $1,500 (digital/social, 1yr, non-exclusive)
- Extended: $2,500-$5,000 (broadcast, global, category-exclusive options)
- Custom Placement: $12,000-$20,000+ (full service with AI regeneration)

## Key Takeaways for SI8 Pricing

### 1. $1,500 Floor is Validated
- **Filmsupply:** $500-$2,500 digital (premium stock)
- **Catch&Release:** $1,500-$4,000 (legal clearance)
- **Getty RM:** $800-$3,000 (rights-managed digital)
- **Consensus:** $1,500 sits in validated premium range

### 2. Broadcast Rights Command Premium
- **Filmsupply:** 2-3x markup (digital $1,500 → broadcast $5,000)
- **Getty:** 3-5x markup for broad distribution
- **SI8 Extended ($2,500-$5,000) aligns with this pattern**

### 3. "Legal Shield" Has Real Value
- **Catch&Release charges $2,000+ just for paperwork**
- **SI8's Chain of Title = similar value proposition**
- Market proves buyers pay premium for risk mitigation

### 4. Custom Services Command 10x+ Premium
- **Stock clip:** $500-$2,500
- **Custom production:** $10,000-$50,000+
- **SI8's $12,000 custom placement = competitive value vs. traditional**

### 5. Three-Tier Structure is Industry Standard
- Getty: RF / RR / RM
- Adobe: Standard / Enhanced / Extended
- SI8: Standard / Extended / Custom Placement
- **Familiar structure = lower buyer friction**
