## Tracking Instructions

**Every Monday Morning (15 min):**
1. Check off completed items from previous week
2. Fill in numerical metrics (filmmakers, catalog, outreach, calls, revenue)
3. Write 1-2 sentence status update per track
4. Add any key learnings (what worked, what didn't)
5. Set up next week's checklist

**Pipedrive Integration:**
- Outreach sent = tracked in Pipedrive "Prospect" stage
- Calls booked = tracked in Pipedrive "Meeting Booked" stage
- Proposals sent = tracked in Pipedrive "Proposal Sent" stage
- Deals closed = tracked in Pipedrive "Won" stage
- Revenue = sum of "Won" deal values

**Red Flags to Watch:**
- Outreach sent but 0 calls booked (messaging problem)
- Calls booked but 0 proposals sent (qualification problem)
- Proposals sent but 0 closes (pricing/value prop problem)
- Supply track stalled (filmmaker friction)
- Demand track stalled (no outreach activity)

**Weekly Review Questions:**
1. Are both tracks progressing, or is one stalled?
2. What's blocking progress this week?
3. What can I do differently next week?
4. Am I spending time on revenue-generating activities, or infrastructure busywork?
