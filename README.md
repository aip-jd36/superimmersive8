Taipei, Taiwan | www.superimmersive8.com

## What We Do

SuperImmersive 8 is building the infrastructure for the AI film ecosystem:

1. **Distribution & Licensing** - Representing AI filmmakers and monetizing their work
2. **AI Creators Fest** - Live events that bring together the AI film community
3. **Curation & Discovery** - Connecting quality AI content with buyers
